# Automotive-Sales-Performance-Dashboard

## Power BI Project | Sales Analytics | Data Visualization

This project analyzes automotive sales performance across multiple manufacturers, vehicle models, and customer segments, focusing on revenue generation, sales volume, and purchasing trends.

The dashboard is designed to provide a clear, executive-level view of how different factors such as vehicle type, transmission, and customer demographics impact overall revenue.

## Key Insights
### High-Level KPIs

Total Revenue Generated: $671.53M

Total Vehicles Sold: 23.91K units

Year-over-Year Revenue Growth: 123.59%

### Revenue Distribution

Male customers contributed 78.49% of total revenue

Female customers accounted for 21.51%

## Product Segmentation

SUVs and Hatchbacks generated the highest revenue across both transmission types

Automatic transmission vehicles consistently outperformed manual vehicles

## Trend Analysis

Revenue steadily increased across quarters

Peak performance observed in the fourth quarter

Black vehicles generated the highest revenue across all periods
